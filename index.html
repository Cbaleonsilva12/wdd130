Puebla, Mexico

Puebla, officially known as Puebla de Zaragoza, is a city in central Mexico. Founded in 1532, it is located about 130 km southeast of Mexico City. The city is renowned for its colonial architecture, including many buildings adorned with Talavera tiles, and its rich history, including the famous Battle of Puebla on May 5, 1862
